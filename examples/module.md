## What is a cell?

A **cell** is the basic structural and functional unit of all living organisms.
Some organisms are single-celled; others, like humans, are made of trillions.

Every cell relies on [[Photosynthesis]] or respiration to obtain energy, and
uses [[Chlorophyll]] (in plants) to capture light.

:::quiz {points: 1}
Q: Roughly how many cells make up an adult human?
- ( ) A few hundred
- ( ) About a million
- (x) Tens of trillions
> The commonly cited estimate is around 30–37 trillion cells.
:::

### Parts of a cell

- The **nucleus** stores genetic material.
- **Mitochondria** produce energy.
- The **cell membrane** controls what enters and leaves.
